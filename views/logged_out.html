RepoRanker

by Ben Wasser
Log in with Github to get started

Rearrange your GitHub repository rankings

Ever wished your favorite projects were higher up in your GitHub profile? RepoRanker is a way to crowdsource the star rankings of your projects.

How it works:

  • Log in with GitHub to allow RepoRanker to access your repo list.
  • You'll be shown other users' repos for you to star.
  • Arrange how you'd like your own repos to be ranked.
  • Your repos will be shown to other users to star, displayed with an algorithm that weights how often it shows the repos by the rankings you set. Eventually, and with enough users, your repos will be starred enough to be sorted in the order you set.
  • The more people who join RepoRanker, the faster your repos will be ranked, so spread the word around.

For your added safety, I don't store your access tokens on the server, but rather have you send them each time you make a request to the site. Don't casually hand out permissions to your accounts, browsers, or phones, as I could just as easily be malicious, careless, or interested in selling your data to third parties (none of which I am, but you can't easily verify that).
